My Project

OpenNerve

Open-source implantable pulse generator platform for bioelectronic medicine research. Includes stimulation leads, recording capabilities, and biomarker sensing. Developed by CARSS with NIH SPARC support.

Hardware, Firmware, C, Open Source

My Project

OpenAVNS

Community-led open source project developing a wearable auricular vagus nerve stimulator. Hardware, firmware, and companion app all freely available under CC-BY-4.0.

Hardware, Firmware, iOS, Open Source

My Project

Susuro Mood Tracker

iOS app for effortless mood tracking with a simple long-press interface. Visualize emotional patterns over time and on a map, with Apple HealthKit integration. Privacy-first: all data stays on your device.

iOS, Swift, HealthKit
